1. 1995 Nebraska Football Team

This team is considered the best team ever in the history of college football by many. They went 12-0 while never having a close game. They defeated teams by an average score of 49-18 and beat 4 teams ranked in the Top 10.

Image from www.espn.com

2. 1971 Nebraska Football Team

This team was 13-0. They were considered the best team of all time, until the 1995 Nebraska Team came around. Their only close game was the "Game of the Century" to #2 ranked Oklahoma. They managed to defeat the number 2, 3, and 4 teams in the final polls.

Image from www.complex.com

3. 1994 Nebraska Football Team

This team ended the season going 13-0. They managed to figure out ways to win even after losing star players Tommie Frazier and Mike Minter. Their last game was 24-17 victory over Miami in the Orange Bowl.

Image from www.journalstar.com

4. 1997 Nebraska Football Team

This team was very special because it happned to be the last team Tom Osbornes would coach at Nebraska. They went 13-0, defeating two teams in the top 8 by an average score of 49-21. They ended up splitting the Nationl Title with Michigan after allowing a close victory of 45-38 over Missouri during the season.

Image from www.pediment.com

5. 1970 Nebraska Football Team

This team finished 11-0-1. The Huskers won the National Title on New Years Day when they defeated LSU 17-12. This was able to happen because top ranked Texas and second ranked Ohio State were both upset. Early on they had a tie with USC, but managed to get through the entire Big 8 with no issues. Some final polls were taken before bowl games, causing the Huskers to split the National Title with Texas and Ohio State.
